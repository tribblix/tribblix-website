--- title: 32-bit support ---

> 32-bit support

As of February 2018, support for 32-bit x86 hardware was removed from illumos. This only affects the kernel; 32-bit applications are still supported.

The last Tribblix version to support 32-bit hardware in any meaningful sense was the 0m20 release. This was unsupported, was many years out of date with no updates or security fixes, and has been completely retired as of early 2025.
