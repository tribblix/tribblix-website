> Tribblix and AI

Tribblix is hand-crafted, artisanal if you like. Those scripts that are provided with Tribblix, and are used to construct Tribblix, are basically what someone would type by hand to do the same purpose, put into scripts to reduce the amount of typing.

As such, AI (specifically, LLMs) is not used to construct Tribblix, and will not be used to make or develop Tribblix in the future.

Tribblix includes many third-party software packages. Those may or may not use AI. Whether they're included in Tribblix is based only on their utility. With the growing use of AI tooling, it's likely that we'll get to the point where some level of AI use is pervasive, and Tribblix is designed to be used and needs applications to fulfil that mission, so applications with varying degrees of AI input will inevitably be present.

(That said, given a choice between a hand-crafted and vibe-coded application of equal utility, the artisanal application will tend to be chosen.)
